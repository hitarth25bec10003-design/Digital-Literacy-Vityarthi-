Stay Safe Online Checklist:
	• Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, and symbols)
	• Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
	• Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts (banking, college portals)
	• Verify links before clicking — especially from unknown emails, WhatsApp, or SMS
	• Check privacy settings on Instagram, Facebook, and other social media accounts
	• Download apps only from official stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store)
	• Log out from shared or college computers after using your accounts
	• Be cautious while sharing personal details like Aadhaar number, college ID, or phone number

UPI Safety :
	• Never share your UPI PIN or OTP 
	• Do not scan unknown QR codes — they may deduct money instead of sending it
	• Check “Pay” vs “Collect” requests carefully before approving UPI payments
	• Avoid using screen-sharing apps when someone claims to help with refunds or payments
